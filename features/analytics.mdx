## **3. Filtering**
You can filter the data by:

1. Source: Easily see where each chat originated, whether from your website, social media, or other platforms.
2. Channel: Email/Chat
3. Category: Categories which are applied to every reply/ conversation (depending on your setting)
4. Feedback: Chats in slack, widget or standalone link can be voted with thumbs up or down. Here you can see aggregated numbers of reviews.
5. Escalation: Yes/No
6. Reference links: Yes/No (Show replies which got a reference link)
