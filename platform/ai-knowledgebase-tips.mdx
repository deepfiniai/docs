<Tip>Create an account at [Fini AI](https://app.usefini.com) to get started</Tip>

<Card title="Add Explanatory Text to Images/Videos/Flowcharts" icon="lightbulb" iconType="duotone">
  LLMs are not great at interpreting images or videos. Including step-by-step instructions with clear bullet points and headers along with visual content for your images, videos and flow charts to ensure Fini can effectively use this information to assist customers.
</Card>

<Card title="Explain Key Terms" icon="lightbulb" iconType="duotone">
  Fini doesn't know the meaning to your internal terms. Make sure to explain the meaning of special terms or acronyms the first time they're used in each article. This will make your content more accessible to Fini and your customers.
</Card>
