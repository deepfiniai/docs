### 6. You are done!
Your training is complete when there is a "green dot" in front of the file. This signifies that your file has been read successfully. You can also click on "Preview data" to look at the contents
![Step 6 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/35d27985-f589-4b35-85d9-4d6bce19c84b/steps/926f399c-9d1d-4b66-b1ad-064c39c124b4/b84d8c6b-1386-437b-b26f-b83700151881.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=66&mark-y=325&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0yNTgmaD0xMDgmZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)
