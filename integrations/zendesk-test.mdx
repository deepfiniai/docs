### Go back to the "Branding" section to turn off streaming

Streaming is a feature that allows users to enjoy a more natural, human-like experience by having words in the response appear one by one, rather than receiving the entire reply all at once. However, this functionality can impact Fini's performance, potentially leading to a decrease in the accuracy of the replies.
