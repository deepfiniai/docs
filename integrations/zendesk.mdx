### 6. Delay Timer
Here you can enter the delay timer (in seconds). For example, If you enter 60 in this box, the bot will wait 60 seconds to respond. This gives you control on how/ when you want to respond to users
![Step 6 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/f5348255-da41-459f-94da-ef33b65d686f/steps/6967fb4b-5afa-4354-8120-8120d9bbd134/5177b886-01b3-4d43-b070-5c75b300be19.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5003&fp-y=0.6884&fp-z=1.3301&w=1200&mark-x=198&mark-y=464&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTYlMkNGRjc0NDImdz04MDUmaD02OCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)



### 7. Enable Public comments
This toggle allows you to switch between internal notes and direct user replies.

* If the toggle is turned OFF then we will create internal notes for your agents.
    
* If the toggle is turned ON then we will reply directly to your users
![Step 7 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/f5348255-da41-459f-94da-ef33b65d686f/steps/5e9bee9a-f949-4bda-9a72-a305b127836e/acc3695f-36e6-431c-8d99-2a6f5b9c5df7.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=298&mark-y=656&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01OSZoPTM2JmZpdD1jcm9wJmNvcm5lci1yYWRpdXM9MTA%3D)
