## Replies Setting

![Step 6 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/87e7d0b2-66fc-42c7-bb49-a8b4eeb06d70/steps/cf739e89-9711-4a02-985f-136cb2c2bbc7/e1fbc51d-c16a-4317-b312-06e7c6494524.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200)

Configure how Fini will handle replies:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Time Delay">
     - To make Fini's response time feel more human-like, you can set a delay in seconds. For instance, a 1-hour delay would be set as `3600` seconds. However for chat seetings we advise to keep it at 0.
![Step 7 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/3b180847-f9c8-4d28-8732-4d32ec680742/steps/19df6f53-e86c-42e1-9ef1-ac641ca73fa4/c1f7229b-ad1b-462d-b392-129bf21569f8.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=604&mark-y=124&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NTcmaD02NiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)

  </Step>
<Step title="Set a Debounce Lag">
    - This feature is useful for chat scenarios where the user sends information in multiple messages. Debounce lag allows the bot to wait for a specified number of seconds before responding. During this time, Fini assesses all incoming messages to generate a more relevant response based on complete understanding of the user issue.
    ![Step 8 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/3b180847-f9c8-4d28-8732-4d32ec680742/steps/968f89a1-ff85-4887-83c3-83c0cebc4902/5d26a549-b61a-48ab-97c4-6b9f2633fa61.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=602&mark-y=227&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NTYmaD02NiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Choose a Reply Type">
    Fini supports two types of replies:
- **Direct Replies**: Direct responses to customers.
- **Internal Comments**: Internal notes visible only to your support agents.

![Step 9 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/3b180847-f9c8-4d28-8732-4d32ec680742/steps/76ab849c-7fff-4255-9d91-2b238c6f6d82/a2d4d133-2c19-4f92-b367-ff60bba460d5.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=609&mark-y=320&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NTQmaD02NSZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Reply Type if Agent Assigned">
    Configure how Fini behaves when a support agent is assigned to a ticket:
- **Reply to End User**: Fini continues engaging with the customer even after agent assignment.
- **Post Internal Comment**: Fini stops communicating directly with the customer and instead posts updates as internal comments for the support team.
- **No Reply**: Fini stops all activity on the ticket, allowing the agent to handle it entirely.


![Step 10 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/3b180847-f9c8-4d28-8732-4d32ec680742/steps/19496728-599e-4b66-b353-5c2d426d3c0b/63e7de7a-abae-4029-8ce1-7942b092f705.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=613&mark-y=415&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NjImaD02NiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)

  </Step>
<Step title="PII Masking">
    To protect **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**, enable PII masking. When enabled, Fini will avoid using personal data (like the user's name, email etc.) in responses, providing a generalized interaction.

**Example**:  
With PII masking:  
*"Hello, how can I assist you today?"*

Without PII masking:  
*"Hello, John, how can I assist you today?"*

![Step 11 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/3b180847-f9c8-4d28-8732-4d32ec680742/steps/f201d020-db0a-48d4-bed6-4b1c2192b95f/38117392-7242-4ab8-a2cd-62bcb77bb795.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=610&mark-y=505&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NTYmaD02NiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Assign Tickets to Fini">
    Automate ticket management by assigning tickets to Fini. This enables further automation (e.g., auto-solving tickets) and analytics within Zendesk.

> **Note**: If you already have specific assignment rules in place, you may keep this option set to **No**.

![Step 12 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/3b180847-f9c8-4d28-8732-4d32ec680742/steps/70337261-346d-4e97-8f6b-800e0aeb804e/b40e535c-15ba-445d-95db-375d2433dca2.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=612&mark-y=607&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NjImaD02NiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
</Steps>

##  **Escalation Setting**

These settings determine how Fini behaves when an issue is escalated to a human agent.

![Step 13 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/87e7d0b2-66fc-42c7-bb49-a8b4eeb06d70/steps/f393cbbc-3e4a-403e-83bf-ebfc0902bb75/ff5180ea-4416-4b19-bbbe-a1e72654a92e.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200)


<Steps>
  <Step title="Action if Escalated">
    Define what happens if Fini encounters a question it cannot answer (e.g., sensitive topics):
    - **Continue Responding**: Fini will continue to engage even after escalation.
    - **No Reply**: Fini stops replying after escalation.
    - **Send Acknowledgement Message**: Fini sends a customizable acknowledgment message to the customer once escalation occurs.

    **Suggested Message**
    > "Thank you for the additional information. This will be included in the ticket for our specialized human agent team."


    ![Step 2 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1e4baf57-fdc4-40b5-99b7-e1984edcb6b8/steps/65b5c3bf-a617-4309-b3fb-1cc943383e4c/ebde1d3b-83f1-4d8d-a325-b61f7de050a6.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=603&mark-y=119&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NDgmaD02NSZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Acknowledgment Message">
     - Customize the message users receive when their query is escalated. 

    **Suggested Message**:  
    > "Thank you for the additional information. This will be included in the ticket for our specialized human agent team."

    ![Step 3 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1e4baf57-fdc4-40b5-99b7-e1984edcb6b8/steps/5eea83b3-18e7-4267-91ce-faa0dbba9c3f/87ffa663-46f8-436a-a0ce-f68041f9df36.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=611&mark-y=204&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NTQmaD0xMjcmZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)
  </Step>
<Step title="Threshold Count for Escalation">
     - Set a **threshold** for the maximum number of replies before escalation. If Fini cannot resolve an issue within a set number of replies (for chat we recommend to put it as high as 100), the ticket is escalated to a human agent.


    ![Step 4 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1e4baf57-fdc4-40b5-99b7-e1984edcb6b8/steps/0a1c2b57-653b-47d7-85b3-bf078f1f48bf/09366f3c-4bf1-4566-b015-04786365df48.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=618&mark-y=369&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NzAmaD02NyZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Message When Escalated via Threshold">
    - Customize the message users receive when the bot reaches the escalation threshold. 

    **Suggested Message When Threshold is Reached**:  
    > "Thank you for your message. Unfortunately, this is outside of my expertise, and I will need to escalate this to our specialized support team. A member of our team will reach out shortly."


    ![Step 5 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1e4baf57-fdc4-40b5-99b7-e1984edcb6b8/steps/6f9d8ded-24ef-400a-abda-8f925acf5078/3113cefa-bb03-4222-927a-90c5fb932712.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=617&mark-y=452&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NjImaD0xODImZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)

## **Categorization Setting**

Once authorization is complete, a new **Categorization** section will appear.


<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Categorization">
    Toggle the **Categorization** setting to allow Fini to automatically categorize tickets based on their content using AI.

![Step 22 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/87e7d0b2-66fc-42c7-bb49-a8b4eeb06d70/steps/ddafc4ac-bf24-4338-8a1f-63485e72170e/e7629876-abad-46af-a315-b7a53d8f94b0.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=1043&mark-y=145&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTMlMkNGRjc0NDImdz03MSZoPTQxJmZpdD1jcm9wJmNvcm5lci1yYWRpdXM9MTA%3D)
  </Step>
<Step title="Select a Categorization Field">
    Select the Zendesk field that you want to use for categorization. This needs to be a Multi-select field.

![Step 23 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/87e7d0b2-66fc-42c7-bb49-a8b4eeb06d70/steps/165539f0-4a3e-4454-97d7-334a170d2921/781aa052-e2d3-4ced-a374-93c669f58b68.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=588&mark-y=230&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTMlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MzImaD02NCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Category Add Type">
    Decide whether new categories should append to each reply or overwrite the previous ones.
