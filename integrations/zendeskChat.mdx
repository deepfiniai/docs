### 7. Choose reply type

We allow you to reply in 2 different ways:
1) Direct replies i.e. direct responses to customers
2) Internal comments i.e. creating internal comments for agents

If you choose to mask the PII we will mask all PII data, and not read/store any PII and also not send them to our ML models/ OpenAI.

![Step 4 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/595a2e75-14a1-49ff-95b8-6475b766657a/steps/e4d8fed6-cd2a-451e-8824-17ff9132950b/4b76b44a-572b-496a-b61f-c89b378056da.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.6137&fp-y=0.5475&fp-z=2.0114&w=1200&mark-x=332&mark-y=404&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTYlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MzUmaD03OCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
