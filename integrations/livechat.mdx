## Replies Setting

![Step 8 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/426b7e59-4517-42f5-8ce7-28034c9ef7bf/fb3f46d3-efed-4104-a27b-7818b0764739.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=17&mark-y=9&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0xOTcmaD00NCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)

Configure how Fini will handle replies:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select time delay">
     - To make Fini's response time feel more human-like, you can set a delay in seconds. However for Chat messages we recommend to keep this at 0.

![Step 9 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/dc62745b-d05c-44fb-a990-11791c1b3d0b/413cf7f9-6651-4d1a-b133-7ec7f393f066.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=566&mark-y=112&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MTUmaD02MSZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Set a Debounce lag">
    - This feature is useful for chat scenarios where the user sends information in multiple messages. Debounce lag allows the bot to wait for a specified number of seconds before responding. During this time, Fini assesses all incoming messages to generate a more relevant response based on complete understanding of the user issue.

![Step 10 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/daae9924-7f60-4309-b281-68173d33db5f/2cbd0959-9867-40c0-8eb7-9ff3bb50bfeb.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=579&mark-y=235&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MjMmaD02MiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Choose a Reply Type">
    Fini supports two types of replies:
- **Direct Replies**: Direct responses to customers.
- **Internal Comments**: Internal notes visible only to your support agents.

![Step 11 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/5fe75041-0ac4-49e0-8c1c-8bc8240e883a/d360af2a-11b8-4c48-94e1-755d43eff828.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=561&mark-y=299&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MTAmaD02MCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="PII masking">
    To protect **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**, enable PII masking. When enabled, Fini will avoid using personal data (like the user's name, email etc.) in responses, providing a generalized interaction.

**Example**:  
With PII masking:  
*"Hello, how can I assist you today?"*

Without PII masking:  
*"Hello, John, how can I assist you today?"*

  </Step>
</Steps>

##  **Escalation Setting**

These settings determine how Fini responds when an issue is escalated to a human agent.
![Step 13 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/e73d122e-196f-482b-a13b-4c7fc7861515/87332dfc-72f7-4882-87df-13b1706c4cea.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=50&mark-y=18&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0yOTAmaD01NiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)


<Steps>
  <Step title="Group on Transfer">
    From the dropdown field select the LiveChat group which will receive the escalated messages 
![Step 5 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/cef76e6e-064e-4e03-b2c8-e9890bfe8c2f/steps/243ba945-f8e0-4772-b9f7-e26b85e2f7fc/45b883bc-7620-42cf-a747-e5c079c9bdb6.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=595&mark-y=122&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NDUmaD02NCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Transfer fail message">
    Define the message that the user will receive if the transfer to a human agent was unsuccessful

    **Suggested Message**
    > "Apologies, my colleagues are not available at the moment, i will escalate your request and someone will reach out to you via e-mail in the shortest time possible"
![Step 14 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/c0c1a2d5-ec8a-41f8-acdc-639db4e86390/a8bf2176-f94f-468f-bbb6-4c14037d6c8e.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=599&mark-y=201&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MjQmaD0xNTEmZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)
  </Step>
<Step title="Action if Escalated">
    Define what happens if Fini encounters a question it cannot answer (e.g., sensitive topics):
    - **Continue Responding**: Fini will continue to engage even after escalation.
    - **No Reply**: Fini stops replying after escalation.
    - **Send Acknowledgement Message**: Fini sends a customizable acknowledgment message to the customer once escalation occurs.

    **Suggested Message**
    > "Thank you for the additional information. This will be included in the ticket for our specialized human agent team."

![Step 15 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/f5238e2c-3b9f-493e-9bd2-906e36a12415/cd48b959-eb0d-4098-b18b-8814e2578672.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=581&mark-y=370&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MjEmaD02MiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)

  </Step>
<Step title="Acknowledgment Message">
    - Customize the message users receive when their query is escalated. 

    **Suggested Message**:  
    > "Thank you for the additional information. This will be included in the ticket for our specialized human agent team."
![Step 16 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/def1e020-67be-400b-be08-afce6a596435/b4ce5b90-237c-4421-a6e2-3211da0b8a43.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=581&mark-y=446&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MDkmaD0xNDYmZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)

  </Step>
<Step title="Threshold Count for Escalation">
    - Set a **threshold** for the maximum number of replies before escalation. If Fini cannot resolve an issue within a set number of replies (e.g., 5 or 10), the ticket is escalated to a human agent.

![Step 17 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/c94b056c-e491-4100-bb12-7f18333821b9/95a18c31-b971-4125-931e-fa17e5a62c14.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=589&mark-y=603&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MjQmaD02MiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
  </Step>
<Step title="Message When Escalated via Threshold">
     - Customize the message users receive when the bot reaches the escalation threshold. 

    **Suggested Message When Threshold is Reached**:  
    > "Thank you for your message. Unfortunately, this is outside of my expertise, and I will need to escalate this to our specialized support team. A member of our team will reach out shortly."

![Step 18 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/90a039ba-8b0c-4029-ab1d-b0aa2bd1d9f8/steps/9b5b8557-9288-4e6c-9d21-c9cc91cbc933/1ecc717e-9b00-4daf-ae50-69dead05db12.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&mark-x=596&mark-y=701&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MzgmaD0xNzQmZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)
  </Step>
</Steps>
